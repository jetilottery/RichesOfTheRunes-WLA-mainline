Riches of the Runes

What fortunes will be revealed in the mystical forest? Let the stones divine your rewards in Riches of the Runes!.

Find 4 or more Rainbow gems and win a prize. These powerful stones may also reveal Rune or Amulet symbols. Collect 4 Rune symbols to trigger the Rune Bonus, and win additional prizes or collect more Amulet symbols. Collect 4 Amulet symbols to trigger the Amulet Bonus, where you may reveal a multiplier up to 10x!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus sign (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus sign (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Riches of the Runes is to find 4 or more Rainbow gems and win a prize.

Press each glowing stone to reveal a Rainbow gem or a Green gem. Each revealed symbol may have a Rune sub-symbol or an Amulet sub-symbol.

Reveal 4 or more Rainbow gems to win a prize based on the prize table. Revealing more Rainbow gems increases the prize amount.

Collect 4 Rune symbols to trigger the Rune Bonus. Select 4 of 8 stones to reveal a rune. If the revealed rune matches a rune on the portal, a prize or an Amulet symbol is awarded.

Collect 4 Amulet symbols to trigger the Amulet Bonus. Select an amulet to reveal a multiplier, which is applied to the total win.

Play ends when all symbols are revealed and all bonus games are completed.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
